Bachelor of Technology - Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering


The programme is designed to make a strong foundation in EXTC that includes electronics and hardware. telecom courses are introduced in the early semesters while non-IT courses in the later stages.Electronics workshops are specially designed courses allowing the students to have mastery in the use of the latest software and hardware technologies. The courses in mathematics, sciences, engineering and humanities enable students to explore the wider applications of technology.
